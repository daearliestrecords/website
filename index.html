About The Label

Good music, happy people.

Da Earliest Records

Da Earliest Records is a dynamic, forward thinking independent record label dedicated to discovering and nurturing fresh talent. With a passion for pushing boundaries, we pride ourselves on creating a platform for artists who are to leave their mark on the music industry.


Founded by music lovers, for music lovers, our mission is to provide a creative space where innovation meets opportunity. From raw, up-and -coming acts to seasoned artists looking for a new path, Da Earliest Records is committed to cultivating authenticy and heloing artits thrive in today's fast-paced music world.


We're not just about the music, we're about the story behind th the socond. At Da Earlist Records, we work closely with our artists, offering a personalised, hands-on approach to every project. Our team of experienced producers, marketers, and creative ensures each artit has the resources they need to succeed.

Join us on this journey as we redefine the future of music, one track at a time.
